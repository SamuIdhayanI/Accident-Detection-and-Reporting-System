Enhancing Road Safety: AI-Powered Accident Severity Detection and Fallen Person Counting System with Immediate Emergency Response

Our Approach

Detection

Use a Deep learning model to on high-resolution images of roads captured by a cctv camera. Identify the pixels that belong to different types of accidents and look for fallen people.

Information gathering

Extract the coordinates and dimensions of the detected accidents from the segmented images. Store the images and the metadata in a database along with the location information obtained from GPS or other sources.

Solution

Send an alert to the nearby ambulance drivers, hospitals, police stations and municipal authorities with the details of the accidents and their locations. Provide a web interface to access the database and monitor the status of the road hazards.
